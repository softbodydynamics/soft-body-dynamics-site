I don't just prompt; I direct.

With a professional background spanning VFX, compositing, photography, filmmaking, design, and development, I approach AI generation as a specialized branch of digital cinematography. Most AI content relies on algorithms to guess the lighting and composition. I use fully posable 3D models together with specific LORAs to dictate it. 

This hybrid pipeline allows for true, granular control over every scene element—from character outfits and exact poses to the precise placement of practical lights and environmental props. Whether the goal is a moody, cinematic narrative or the bland, unfiltered photorealism of an amateur smartphone camera, the result is consistent, deliberate, and undeniably real.
